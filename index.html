About Me

As a seasoned Software Engineer with over two decades of global experience, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and cross-cultural perspective to every project. Currently serving as a Software-Magier at medigital GmbH in Basel, Switzerland, I specialize in crafting innovative healthcare software solutions that make a real difference in people's lives.

My journey in technology has taken me across continents - from Switzerland to Germany, Malaysia to Singapore, and Iran - allowing me to work with diverse teams and tackle complex challenges in various cultural contexts. This international experience has shaped my approach to problem-solving and reinforced my belief in the power of technology to bridge gaps and create meaningful solutions.

I'm passionate about full-stack development and have a particular interest in healthcare technology, modern web applications, and scalable architecture. Whether it's implementing secure patient data systems or optimizing application performance, I focus on delivering solutions that are not just technically sound but also user-centric and impactful.
